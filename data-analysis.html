The Challenge

E-commerce environments generate massive arrays of transactional records, but identifying granular financial leakages and specific product-category drivers requires multi-dimensional filtering across price, cost, and region.

The Approach

Engineered data-mining operations inside Google Colab to clean, filter, and transform over 3,500 rows of transactional metrics, then constructed financial tracking equations to compute return margins and generate comparative charts of regional and seasonal trends.

Key Questions This Analysis Answers

  • Which product categories drive the highest profit margins versus which sit as dead stock?
  • How do operational logistics costs erode net returns on a per-item basis?
  • Where do seasonal volume spikes create inventory restocking risk?
  • Which regions deliver the strongest profitability-to-cost ratio for resource allocation?

The Outcome

A formalized corporate-level evaluation report delivering precise, data-driven recommendations on product inventory restructuring and regional resource distribution — open the executive PDF for the full breakdown of charts and figures.

The Challenge

National financial sustainability is profoundly intertwined with public mental health, environmental wellness, and institutional governance — but un-integrated datasets mask these critical correlations from policymakers.

The Approach

Consolidated and mapped macro-historical datasets from the World Happiness Report and Corruption Perceptions Index in Python, conducting exploratory data analysis, correlation matrix mappings, and multi-variable evaluations across continents.

Key Questions This Analysis Answers

  • Does a country's Corruption Perceptions Index correlate with its Happiness Index over time?
  • Which institutional integrity indicators move together with public well-being scores?
  • How did the corruption-to-happiness relationship shift across the 2015–2020 window?
  • Which continents show the strongest, and weakest, governance-to-wellbeing link?

The Outcome

An extensive global insight report highlighting policy-level conclusions and socio-economic frameworks, validating data visualization competency beyond pure commercial use cases — open the executive PDF for the full regression plots and country comparisons.
